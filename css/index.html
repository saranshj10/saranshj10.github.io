ISME Conference

Present ISME Conference on Mechanical Engineering is the Nineteenth in the series of confereces organised by Indian Society of Mechanical Engineers (ISME). The conference will be held during December 20th-22nd 2018 at Dr B. R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology Jalandhar. To keep abreast of latest developments in the industry, a platform is set-up under the aegis of ISME to organize conferences. The purpose of this conference is to bring together the Mechanical Engineering community to explore, disseminate and strenghten initiatives in new directions under the broad areas of Machine Design, Materials and Manufacturing, Thermal Engineering and Industrial Engineering. The conference is aimed at enabling sustainable development through cutting edge technologies of Mechanical Engineering.

Dr B. R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology Jalandhar

The Institute is named after Bharat Ratna Dr B R Ambedkar, founder of Indian Constitution. The institute came into existence in the year 1988 as Regional Engineering college on a beautiful campus of about 154 acres close to the heart of Jalandhar city. The institute obtained the status "Institute of National Importance" by Act of Parliament in 2007. The institute is located on the G.T. Road Amritsar bye-pass at a distance of 15 km from Jalandhar Bus Stand, 12 km from Jalandhar City Railway Station, and 18 km from Jalandhar Cantt. Railway Station. The Institute is well connected to National Capital through rail and road link. For more information & details about institute activities, click here

Department of Mechanical Engineering

The Department of Mechanical Engineering was established in 1990 and has grown as one of the dynamic departments of the Institute over the last three decades. The Department offers B.Tech, M.Tech and PhD programmes. The B.Tech program is accredited by NBA. With a dedicated team consisting of highly qualified and experienced faculty and staff in all streams of Mechanical Engineering, the department aims at providing education and research of world class level. The Department is funded by many prestigious R&D grants under FIST, Extra Mural Research, Young Scientist Schemes by SERB, DST and TEQIP. Call for papers/Posters

The organizers invite papers from students, faculty, scientists and researchers in academia and industry to present the results of their research and development efforts in Mechanical Engineering. Papers must report original academic or industrial research in any of the tracks not limited to but relevant to the conference.
